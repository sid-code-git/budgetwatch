Submit a Town

Know a town that deserves a closer look? We prioritize towns with no local newspaper and a publicly available budget document. You don't need to be an expert — just a tip and a link.

Usually found on the town's official website under "Finance," "Budget," or "Town Documents." A PDF is fine.

Optional: add a title or affiliation, e.g. "Jane Smith, Harlan resident."

Note: Submitting a town doesn't guarantee a report. We review every submission and prioritize by data availability, news-desert status, and capacity. We'll follow up if we need more.
