Your money. Your right to know.

The average American household pays thousands in local taxes every year. Where does it go? Most towns publish a budget — but it's a 200-page PDF filed with the town clerk that almost nobody reads. We read it for you.

Our full mission

How it works

  1. We obtain the official published budget document.
  2. We run five quantitative checks against prior years and peer towns.
  3. Every flag becomes a question to raise — never an accusation.
  4. Every figure is cited to the source page.
